About Strawbz.

Strawbz is about delight. Bountiful, fresh red strawberries served in a cup with melted chocolate poured over from a cascading chocolate fountain. It’s a show stopper, and titillating for tastebuds. Plus, it’s a healthier indulgence than most.
Hire us for events and functions. From corporate events to birthdays, anniversaries, school events and weddings. Anything that requires a standout experience it creates a buzz with an upmarket, celebratory and elegant experience.
You can also find us on Wednesday evenings at the night market in the summer months, at the Oranjezicht City Farm Market.
